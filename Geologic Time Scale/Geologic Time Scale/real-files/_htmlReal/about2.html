ABOUT US

Paleontologas Inc. © is a fossil-hunting, paleoecology-oriented company that is based in Ulaan Baatar, Mongolia. Our goal is to contribute to the overall progress of the paleontological world, by discovering possibly new, undiscovered fossils in places where fossil hunting have never been done before. Know more about us by reading the information below!

Paleontologas logo ©

Company Establisment

Paleontologas Inc. © is a paleoecology-based fossil-hunting company established in the year 2030, with the main headquarter located in Ulaan Baatar, Mongolia, East Asia. Our main goal is to contribute to the fascinating & bizarre world of paleontology, whether it be by discovering new ancient fossils of plants, animals, fungi, protista, etc., by publishing research papers, building museums, or just by giving out educational facts about the fascinating field of paleontology.

Employees of Company

Our company has recruited over 350 skilled, sharp-minded, and goal-oriented employees, with over 80 nationalities, all tolerantly working and cooperating with each other to make sure we reach goals that can be beneficial to humanity in some sort of way. And we are definitely looking forward to recruit some more; as we expand our headquarters throughout the globe and try to contribute as much as we can to this gorgeous field of study.

Fossil Hunting

During the 3 years that this company has been run, we have discovered over 430 fossil fragments, with 23 of them being confirmed and agreed by scientists all over the world to be entirely new species. Most of our fossil excavation sites are located in remote and barren areas with very limited human activity, ensuring that no civilians might be disturbed or hurt in the excavating/burrowing process. You may even help contribute in finding these fossils through fossil-hunting just for fun, with required permission if you're under the age of 17.
